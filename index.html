Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reasoning model like o3 or Claude Opus 4?

+

Not at all! nterm's reasoning engine works with any AI model, enabling chain-of-thought processing even with standard models like Llama 3/4, Claude 3.5/3.7, Gemini 2.0, etc.

What's the difference between Open Source and SaaS versions?

+

The open-source version provides AI terminal assistance with the self management of the system. Our SaaS platform adds Planning Mode, multi-agent teams, remote operations, team collaboration, and managed AI with advanced reasoning capabilities.

How does the Planning Mode work with approval workflows?

+

Our Master Reasoning Agent analyzes your request, creates a detailed execution plan with risk assessment and cost estimation. High-risk operations require approval from designated team members or executives before execution.

Can I deploy different specialist agents for my team?

+

Yes! Deploy DevOps, Security, Data, Cloud, and Custom specialist agents. Each agent has domain expertise and works together under the Master Reasoning Agent's coordination.

How secure is remote access across multiple servers?

+

Enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, audit logging, and role-based access control. All connections use industry-standard SSH protocols with additional security layers.

What happens if an agent makes a critical mistake?

+

Multiple safety layers: risk assessment, approval workflows, rollback procedures, checkpoint validation, and confirmation steps for destructive operations. Plus comprehensive audit trails for compliance.

How does pricing work - do I need my own AI API keys?

+

Open Source: Use your own API keys. SaaS: We provide managed AI with usage-based billing. Starter ($9/mo) includes 1,000 AI calls with option to increase credits. Enterprise has unlimited usage.

Can I migrate from open source to the SaaS platform?

+

Absolutely! We provide migration tools to import your workflows, configurations, and team settings. Most migrations complete in under 30 minutes with zero downtime.

How many team members can collaborate on workflows?

+

Starter: 5 users, Professional: 25 users, Enterprise: Unlimited. Features include real-time collaboration, task assignment, approval workflows, and team-specific agent configurations.

Can I create custom agents for my specific use cases?

+

Professional and Enterprise plans support custom agent creation with your own training data, specialized tools, and domain-specific knowledge bases. Deploy them alongside our standard specialist agents.

How does the cost estimation and billing work?

+

Before executing complex workflows, you see estimated costs and duration. Billing is transparent: base subscription + usage-based charges for AI calls, workflow executions, data processing, and compute time. No surprise bills.

What level of support do you provide?

+

Starter: Community support, Professional: Email support with 24-hour response, Enterprise: Dedicated support team, phone support, custom training, and dedicated customer success manager.

Can I run NTerm on-premises for sensitive environments?

+

Enterprise customers can deploy NTerm on-premises or in their private cloud with the same SaaS features. Includes dedicated deployment support and ongoing maintenance.

How does the QR-based setup work across devices?

Scan a QR code with your phone, log into our secure web interface, enter your credentials once. This automatically configures terminal access across all your devices - laptops, servers, containers, or IoT devices.

What AI models power the different agents?

+

We use the best model for each task: GPT-4 Turbo for planning, Claude 3 Opus for reasoning, specialized models for security scanning, and custom fine-tuned models for domain-specific tasks. You don't need to manage any of this.
